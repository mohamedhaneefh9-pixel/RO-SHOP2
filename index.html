Frequently Asked Questions

Water Purifier FAQ

How often should I change my RO water purifier filters?

We recommend replacing the pre-filter sediment and pre-carbon cartridges every 6 to 8 months depending on raw input water quality. The main RO membrane typically lasts between 1.5 to 2 years under regular domestic usage.

What is a TDS controller and why is it important?

A TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) controller allows adjustment of dissolved minerals in purified water. Raw ground-water in Dindigul can have high TDS. A TDS controller ensures minerals like Calcium and Magnesium are retained in a safe, healthy proportion rather than being completely stripped.

Do you provide free delivery and installation in Dindigul?

Yes, we provide completely free delivery and expert mounting installation across all areas in Dindigul. A service technician will set up the unit, run leak checks, and calibrate the TDS levels in your presence.

What is covered under the Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)?

Our AMC plans cover two filter cartridge replacements, three scheduled servicing visits, electrical spares coverage (SMPS power adaptor/pump motor repair), and priority breakdown repairs with zero visiting charges.
